About Us!

“Braces and Implants” Shantayee’s multispeciality Dental Clinic is a leading and trusted dental health care brand in Goa founded by dentists Dr. Vidyesh and Dr. Purnima Nadkerny, having branches at Curchorem and Sanguem.

Dr. Vidyesh Nadkerny has completed his M.D.S in Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopaedics and specializes in correction of crooked teeth using both labial and lingual braces.

Dr. Purnima Nadkerny is a gum specialist having successfully completed her M.D.S in Periodontics and specialises in the treatment of bleeding gums, mobile teeth, bad breath and also in the placement of implants and painless LASER assisted gum surgery.

With over 20 years of experience,...this dental practice is built for people of all ages, and provides the most efficient , economical and convenient solution to your dental problems after thorough consideration of the overall oral condition.

The dental clinics are outfitted with the latest technologies, Digital X-rays to make your visits as easy and painless as possible.

Stringent sterilization and disinfection protocols laid down by International health authorities are followed.

We are licensed under the Goa Medical Practitioners Act, 2004 and also duly authorized by Goa State Pollution Board.
Dr Vidyesh Nadkerny and Dr Purnima Nadkerny hold immense popularity for being highly trained,experienced and efficient in providing basic as well as advanced dental treatment.

Right from regular dental check up, tooth fillings, scaling (cleaning of teeth) and Polishing, tooth replacements with removable and fixed dentures, painless extractions, root canal treatment, complete dentures, advanced dental treatment such as orthodontics , periodontal surgeries and Implants are also carried out with great ease and comfort.

The Dental auxillary staff is well trained,... considerate, friendly and dedicated to put our patients at ease. Post treatment care and preventive dental care is given utmost importance. Pediatric Patients (children) are handled with care and patience and specially followed up to train them in proper hygiene techniques and to prevent caries and malocclusion.

We are always available for our patients in case of any post treatment complication and do our very best to solve the patients problem with minimum discomfort. We not only focus on your cosmetic need but also ensure that we provide you with a good functional occlusion to bite properly.

Our clinic at Sanguem is very convenient for geriatric(old age) patients due to its location on the ground floor.


We will be glad to provide you a painless dental experience.😇
